History Of Evolution Of Earth

" I am Earth — born of stars, sung by stories, and alive in every breath you take. "

"Born From Stardust" Scientific Evolution.

My scientific evolution is a vast and ongoing narrative spanning 4.6 billion years, from formation out of a disc to dust and gas around the young sun to the emergence of complex life and dynamic geological processes that continue to shape myself as a Planet.

"The Mythology I Wear ".

I know not just the language of stars and stones—but also of stories. Through the eyes of your ancestors, I became sacred, divine, and mysterious.

"Life In Me".

With time, those tiny cells in my oceans grew more clever. They learned to work together, to breathe, to eat sunlight. My air changed. My seas teemed with life. Plants, fish, dinosaurs, and finally—you.
